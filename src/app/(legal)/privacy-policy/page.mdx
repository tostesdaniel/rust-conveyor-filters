# Privacy Policy

Last updated on August 8, 2024

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as when you create an account, create filters, or contact us for support. This includes your name and email address.

## 2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain our service.
- Improve, personalize, and expand our service.
- Communicate with you when you submit a feedback and to provide you with updates and other information relating to the service.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our service, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

## 4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use the following types of cookies:

- **Strictly Necessary Cookies**: These cookies are essential for the operation of our service. They include cookies set by [Clerk](https://clerk.com/) when you sign in or sign up. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information and are required for our app to function.
  - **First Party Cookies**: `_session`, `_client_uat`
  - **Third Party Cookies**: `_cfduid` (set by Cloudflare for security and performance)

## 5. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from unauthorized and disclosure.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
